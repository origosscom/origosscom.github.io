for enterprises

Digital
Transformation

The business success of an enterprise is determined by the quality of its business software, which is based on their effective development and operability. In addition to reducing the ideas-to-market time required for new features to appear, scalability, security and resilience of applications must be ensured too. For this reason digital transformation, modernization of business applications and introduction of modern business applications are in focus.

Technically it requires a cloud platform of some sort. There are numerous best-practice solutions, so it is ... irrelevant which vendor’s product or service gets chosen in the end. The point is on well-designed processes, properly selected functional components and automation. We have significant experience and expertise in this field, so we can assist a lot in developing a cloud native strategy.

Cloud technology expertise and acceptance of the DevOps approach will be getting stronger within the enterprise. Trust in their benefits and commitment to their application will also increase. Based on this knowledge the development of cloud native strategy as well as digital transformation becomes available, also the specific implementation steps can be deduced.

Finally, a containerized platform will form that the client will be able to operate, where we optionally provide support to this activity. Software developer suppliers will have to adapt to this new platform. This significantly reduces both the enterprise's exposure and operating costs.

Last but not least, we enable our clients' developers and operators to have access to the expertise and experience of our recognized specialists in the field, which ensures a continuous knowledge transfer.

for startups

Scaling up
Business

Many startup companies' core product is a digital service. They can only be successful only if their product is also successful, meaning their clients are willing to pay money for its use and the company can serve this demand in the right manner.

During the first years, the focus is on feature development. It is typically a greenfield activity and it is almost certain that it will follow the DevOps methodology where software modules are built on a microservices architecture and ... the applications get deployed as containers.

Once the product is launched on the market, the focus will move to customer acquisition. However, successful market operations have technical conditions too. On one hand they need to ensure that they can serve more and more clients. On the other hand, the reliability of the service must be guaranteed. This requires a horizontally scalable, resilient and efficiently operable platform. Functional development will stay in focus, as short ideas-to-market cycles are critical to competition. This requires well designed and automated development and deployment processes.

This is where Origoss Solutions comes into play. As an extension of the company's DevOps team we help to improve the development and the operational processes. Dead ends can be avoided and best-practice solutions get to be used thanks to our expertise and experience. We ensure that the staff can continue to focus on feature development and market acquisition.

Our proposal is a continuous collaboration as a service. We would participate in the recurring architectural meetings, validate various concepts with fast PoC projects and take part in platform development if needed. We provide support and customized training too. This kind of cooperation results in a reliable and efficient platform that can be scaled out as the market presence grows. This can ensure the success of the digital product and the company.

Professional
Services

According to our strategy we keep our professional focus as narrow as possible, although within this narrow area we dive into technologies as deeply as possible. A typical IT systems integrator company does just the opposite. These companies have a loyal client base and undertake any complex IT project for them if required. However there may be several areas where they do not have the appropriate competence, therefore they have to involve a professional subcontractor company.

The cloud native ecosystem is a typical area where most large IT systems integrator companies lack ... appropriate human resources to implement projects in-house. The novelty of the topic, the relatively large number of systems and the extremely rapid development of the ecosystem together result in this situation. However, clients are increasingly expecting this kind of expertise.

Our professional focus is on the cloud native ecosystem. Including Kubernetes itself, the ecosystem around it, and the most popular enterprise Kubernetes distributions and managed Kubernetes services. Origoss Solutions provides consulting, systems integration, systems support and training services in this area.
