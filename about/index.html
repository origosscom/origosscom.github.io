Positions

Cloud native ecosystem is relatively young. Even the best Kubernetes experts just have up to 3-5 years of experience. Because of this, it is very difficult to define junior, mid, and senior experience levels and make it even more difficult to distinguish between them.

In a supportive environment provided by the Origoss Solutions team, cloud native expertise can be gained very quickly. That is why the lack of cloud native experience is not an exclusionary factor when hiring a new colleague. Soft skills are more important, and it is a major advantage if the applicant has experience in consulting, operations support, system integration or software development in another field of informatics. In addition to the cloud native experts, we are also looking for candidates who can become a cloud native expert in a short period of time. No matter how much experience you have, you will find challenges and you will be able to improve with us.
